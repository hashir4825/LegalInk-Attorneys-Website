ABOUT LEGALINK ATTORNEYS

Trusted Legal Counsel Across Litigation, Advisory and Regulatory Matters

Legalink Attorneys is a Pakistan-based law firm led by partners with experience across litigation, corporate advisory, regulatory compliance, and banking law. The firm supports multinational corporations, public-sector entities, and high-net-worth individuals on complex, high-stakes matters, including cross-border mandates. The firm’s practice includes structuring and negotiating commercial contracts, corporate governance and compliance, joint ventures, anti-money laundering, foreign direct investment, and public-private partnerships. Legalink Attorneys also represent clients before lower courts, tribunals, and tax authorities, and prepare writ petitions and appeals before the High Courts of Pakistan
