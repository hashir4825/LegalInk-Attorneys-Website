Firm Overview

Legalink Attorneys supports multinational corporations, public-sector entities, and high-net-worth individuals on complex, high-stakes matters, including cross-border mandates.

The firm’s practice includes structuring and negotiating commercial contracts, corporate governance and compliance, joint ventures, anti-money laundering, foreign direct investment, and public-private partnerships.

The firm also represents clients before lower courts, tribunals, and tax authorities, and prepares writ petitions and appeals before the High Courts of Pakistan.
